Deflated Swissohedron

This is a variant of the Swissohedron with the crosses a bit closer to the centre of the polyhedron. See more about corossohedra here.

Depending whether you interprete the crosses that consist of 5 squares as concave equilateral dodecagons or as 2 intersecting rectangles with a ratio of 1:3, this polyhedron either consists of 12 crosses and 32 congruent equilateral triangles or of 12 crosses, 8 small equilateral triangles and 8 bigger equilateral triangles. In case of the latter this polyhedron is isogonal. One can also see this polyhedron as a cuboctahedron with a tetrahemihexahedron attached to each triangle.

The model was built in 2021 and the smallest edge length is 2.5 cm (1 inch). The diameter is around 8.5 cm (3.3 inch).
