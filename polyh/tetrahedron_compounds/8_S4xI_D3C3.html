Compound of Eight Tetrahedra

This is a compound of 8 tetrahedra that has all the symmetries of a cube. I built this model in October 2022 and the height is around 12 cm (4.7 inch) This fundamental compound is a rigid compound in the sense that this isn't a compound with any rotation freedom, unlike the compound of eight tetrahedra shown here.
