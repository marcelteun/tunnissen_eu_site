Compound of Six Tetrahedra

Here is another uniform compound of uniform polyhedra. After completing a big model that took me almost a year I wanted to build a small model. I like smaller models better than the big ones. They are easier to store and take with you and they are more sturdy.

In the completed model each colour is the classical Stella Octangula consisting of two tetrahedra. A Stella Octangula can be contained exactly in a cube and this model is directly related to the classical compound of three cubes.

The model isn't very complex and it took me only one week to build. I finished it in the late spring of 2018
