Rigid Compound of 6 Cubes

This is the rigid compound of 6 cubes with cube symmetry. After finishing a compound of 12 cubes I needed a small project that I could finish quickly before my wife was due. That became this small model (to get an idea about the size I put a pair of tweezers in the front). On the picture above you look more or less into an order 4-axis.
