Interactive 3D Models of Polyhedra

On this page you find links to animations and interactive 3D models of polyhedra. Previously there were VRML and X3D models here. For these you needed a plugin. In 2024 I removed those, since all models are available in JavaScript. If JavaScript is enabled in your browser and you have 3D support, then nothing extra is needed.
