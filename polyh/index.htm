Tune's Polyhedra Pages

A big part of my spare time is put into my interest of polyhedron models. This site contains the following:

  • Service to show local OFF files in 3D player
  • A collection of interactive models and animations of polyhedra.
  • A collection of some cube compounds I built.
  • A collection of some Uniform Compound Polyhedra.
  • A random collection of other models I built.

Links

A very extensive amount of information you can find on George Hart's Encyclopedia of Polyhedra. Otherwise there is a good page with links to polyhedral web sites at U. Mikloweit's website, which is really worth seeing as well b.t.w.
